Samuele Centorrino

Welcome to my webpage.

I am a Computational Economics Expert at the International Monetary Fund. I was previously an Associate Professor of Economics (with tenure) at Stony Brook University.

I hold a PhD from the Toulouse School of Economics, obtained under the supervision of Professor Jean-Pierre Florens.

My primary research interest is in Econometric Theory. In particular, I work on estimation and inference in regression models with endogenous regressors.

Research interests:

  • Econometrics
  • Nonparametric Statistics
  • Behavioral Economics and Finance
